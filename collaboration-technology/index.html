What to Put in Your Wiki

  • Documentation about an event or campaign
    • Volunteer roles
    • Lists of supplies
    • Recruitment text
    • Schedules
    • After-event evaluation for next year's organizers

What Else?

  • How-to's for organizers
  • Meeting minutes
  • Insurance policy numbers
  • Any text that doesn't need lots of formatting

What Doesn't Go in a Wiki?

  • Spreadsheety things
  • Work that requires design
  • Private information about donors and volunteers

Breaking it down

Each part of the project is a to-do list.

Each list has many to-do items.

Give each item a due date and an owner.
