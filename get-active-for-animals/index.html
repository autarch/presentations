What's the Goal?

What's Not the (End) Goal?

  • Veganism
  • Eliminate foie gras/rodeos/fur coats

These are just intermediate steps.

Who Can Help Animals the Most?

  • Meat-eaters
  • Leather wearers
  • Users of soap, shampoo, makeup, etc.
  • Circus attendees

Don't worry, you can help too!

Consumers, Consumers, Consumers

  • Consumers buy the products that require animal abuse
  • Consumers can stop buying these products

High Impact Activism

  • Most animals used by humans are used for food
  • Most food animals are raised in factory farms
  • Over 2 billion chickens in the US alone
