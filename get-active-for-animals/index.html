What to Do?

The Simplest Possible Thing

  • Leafleting is simple
  • Leafleting is cheap
  • Leafleting is easy

Places to Leaflet

  • Colleges
  • High schools
  • Concerts
  • Outdoor festivals

Leafleting Basics

  • Smile
  • Make eye contact
  • Say hello
  • Hand people the leaflet

Tabling

  • A lot like leafleting, but with more stuff
  • Can show videos
  • Can offer more literature

Where to Table

  • Colleges, especially orientation, other events
  • Outdoor events - May Day, Pride, etc.
  • Farmer's market
  • Anywhere lots of people are strolling (not traveling)

More Ideas

  • Vegan camping trip
  • Vegan shopping tour
  • Food giveaway
  • Public film screening
  • Vegan cooking class
  • Educational speaker
  • A series of (fortunate) events (CAA's Veg Week)
  • Veg Fest!

Reading is Fundamental

  • Eating Animals by Jonathan Safran Foer
  • Meat Market by Erik Marcus
  • Animal Liberation by Peter Singer
  • http://www.veganoutreach.org/advocacy/ - Essays by Vegan Outreach

Handling FAQs

  • Keep your sense of humor
  • Seek common ground
  • Focus on animal suffering, not abstractions
  • Listen to the other person, ask questions
  • It's okay to say "I don't know"
  • Don't engage with trolls

Common Topics

  • Other animals eat each other. Why can't we eat them?
  • What about plants? Don't plants feel pain?
  • What will we do with all the chickens if everyone goes vegan?
  • I'd like to be a vegetarian but it's too hard
  • I'd like to be vegan but I could never give up cheese (or ice cream, or pizza, or lutefisk)
  • Humans are at the top of the food chain
  • Is it wrong to have a dog or cat?
  • What about medical experimentation on animals?

Stay Healthy

  • Be a good role model for veganism
  • Take a B12 supplement
  • Make sure you get enough calcium, iron, and Vitamin D
  • Read Vegan for Life
