Speaking Skills Are Important

I speak at a lot of conferences, and I consistently give highly rated presentations.

I've been doing this for nearly 15 years, and my first presentations were absolutely terrible!

I have no special skills. I've just practiced a lot.

In Addition to Practicing

  • Watch speakers you think are powerful
  • Analyze what they're doing
  • Why are they moving? Persuasive? Powerful?

A Few Tips Before We Start

  • Minimize ums/ahs
  • Don't be afraid to pause
  • Relax your body
  • Don't fidget
  • Make eye contact
  • Use your voice

And a Few More

  • Use your upper body for gestures
  • Move around, but not too much
  • Stay on message
  • Tell sincere personal stories
  • Be passionate
