Building Power

  • Conversion versus Accomodation or Coercion
  • People embrace our message
  • Most people are at least somewhat sympathetic to our message
  • We want them to join us
  • Note that we don't need them all the way on our end to reach a tipping point
  • But there are barriers to joining us
  • Perception (vegans are all jerks)
  • Practical barriers (what will I eat?)
  • Social (what will my friends and family think?)
  • Avoid polarization - don't push people away
  • Tipping point leads to massive change

Strategic Implications

  • Focus on growing the movement
  • Focus on lowering barriers to entry

CAA's Approach

  • Strong organization

Defining Violence

  • Physical violence
  • Hostile behavior

Violence Hurts Us

  • People stop listening
  • Alienates supporters

  • Government repression

Integrity and Openness

  • Don't rely on secrecy for effectiveness
  • Be honest
  • Don't disseminate misinformation

Egalitarianism Builds a Movement

  • Delegate authority
  • Encourage volunteers to become leaders
  • Make best use of our resources
  • Build movement capacity

Be Good Role Models

  • Approachable, welcoming, fun
  • Move towards veganism
  • Soft approach
